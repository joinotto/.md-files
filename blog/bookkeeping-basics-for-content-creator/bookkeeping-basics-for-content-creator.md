### **2. Business Expenses**

To understand your profitability and qualify for tax deductions, tracking expenses is vital. Common expenses for creators include:

*   **Equipment and Software:** Cameras, lighting, microphones, editing software.
*   **Production Costs:** Props, set design, or outsourcing video editing.
*   **Marketing & Ads:** Social media promotions, website hosting, or SEO tools.
*   **Travel Costs:** Flights, lodging, or local travel for shoots.
*   **Professional Services:** Accountants, graphic designers, or legal consultations.
*   **Learning & Development:** Online courses, workshops, or memberships.

**How Otto Helps:** Otto automatically imports your transactions, categorizes expenses, and stores digital receipts. Just snap a photo of a bill or receipt, and Otto does the rest — ensuring every deductible expense is logged.
