FAQs
----

**1. What are the payment methods?**

Payments are processed via PayPal, ensuring secure and fast transactions. Note that PayPal may charge service fees.

**2. How does the approval process work?**

Once you submit your application on the Travelstart affiliate page, expect an approval decision within 3–5 days.

**3. What content is required for participation?**

Influencers are encouraged to create engaging content that promotes Travelstart's services, like posts, reviews, and videos featuring travel tips and destinations.

**4. How are commissions tracked and paid?**

Commissions are tracked via a unique affiliate link provided upon approval, and payments are made monthly based on your earnings.

**5. Is there support available for affiliates?**

Yes, Travelstart provides dedicated affiliate managers for support, along with resources to enhance your promotional efforts.
