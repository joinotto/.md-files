### Are digital signatures on contracts legally valid?



Yes. Digital signatures meet legal standards under laws like the U.S. ESIGN Act and the EU’s eIDAS Regulation, making electronically signed legal contracts enforceable when parties consent and the signature’s authenticity is verifiable.

### Can a contract be modified after signing?



Yes, but any changes to a legal contract require a formal amendment signed by all original parties. Both sides must agree and sign a new addendum or updated version to keep the contract enforceable.
