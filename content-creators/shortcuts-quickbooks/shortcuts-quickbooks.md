Essential QuickBooks Shortcuts
------------------------------

Now, let’s dive into the most crucial QuickBooks shortcuts you need to know.

### 1. Ctrl + N – Create New Transaction

This shortcut quickly brings up the window to add a new transaction, whether it's an invoice, sales order, or purchase order.

### 2. Ctrl + Q – QuickReport

Generate a quick report for any item selected, helping you analyze your data swiftly.

### 3. Ctrl + I – Create Invoice

Jump directly to invoice creation, allowing for faster sales processing.

### 4. Ctrl + E – Edit Selected Transaction

Facilitates quick edits to any highlighted transaction, ensuring accurate bookkeeping.

### 5. Ctrl + F – Find Transactions

Open the find window to search through transactions efficiently.

### 6. F2 – Product Information

This key will provide you with details about the current company file, including lists of users and settings.

### 7. F3 – Search

The search feature comes in handy when you need to find specific information within QuickBooks.

### 8. Ctrl + D – Delete Transaction

Easily delete a highlighted transaction quickly without navigating through menus.

### 9. Ctrl + M – Memorize Transaction

Save and automate repetitive transactions with this convenient shortcut.

### 10. Alt + M – Memorized Transactions List

Open your memorized transaction list with ease, saving you from manually searching for them.

### 11. Ctrl + H – Transaction History

Check the history of the selected transaction, which helps in tracking back and forth.

### 12. Ctrl + R – Record Payment

Record customer payments in a fraction of the time it would normally take.

### 13. Alt + C – Create New Customer

Quickly add a new customer to your QuickBooks without having to navigate through multiple screens.

### 14. Ctrl + Shift + A – Add New Account

This shortcut opens the account setup window to create a new account.

### 15. Ctrl + Alt + 1 – Company Preferences

Access the company preferences to make quick adjustments as needed.
