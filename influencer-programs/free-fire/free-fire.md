What is the Free Fire Influencer Program?
-----------------------------------------

The Free Fire Influencer Program is an initiative by Garena, aimed at building a community of influencers who create engaging content centered around the popular battle royale game, Free Fire. By joining this program, influencers can receive support from Garena, including promotional materials, opportunities to participate in events, and the chance to connect with other creators.

Access to Exclusive Content: Get early access to new game features, skins, and events to share with your followers.

Community Engagement: Connect with like-minded individuals and grow your network within the gaming community.

Application Requirements

To become a member of the Free Fire Influencer Program, you need to meet certain criteria, which can vary based on the region. Here are the general requirements:

Minimum Follower Count

You must have at least 10,000 total followers combined across all your social media platforms. This ensures that you have a sizable audience to engage with.

Content Creation History

At least 8 Free Fire videos must be posted in the last 30 days. This demonstrates your activity and commitment to creating gaming content.

Videos should ideally showcase gameplay, tips, and tricks, or entertaining moments from your matches.

Engagement Metrics

It's advantageous to have an average of at least 10,000 views per video. High engagement is crucial as it reflects your influence in the gaming community.
