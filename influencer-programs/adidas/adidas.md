1. How the Adidas Influencer Program Works
------------------------------------------

Affiliate Program Structure

Adidas provides an affiliate program where influencers can earn commissions by driving sales through unique affiliate links. Here’s a step-by-step breakdown:

1.   Sign Up: Interested influencers can apply to join the Adidas affiliate program through their website.
2.   Receive Links: Once accepted, influencers receive personalized affiliate links to promote products.
3.   Engage Your Audience: By sharing these links on social media or blogs, influencers attract followers who may purchase Adidas products.
4.   Earn Commissions: Influencers earn a commission on each sale made through their affiliate links, incentivizing them to drive traffic effectively.
