What is the Aerie Influencer Program?
-------------------------------------

Aerie, a popular lingerie and apparel brand from American Eagle, has cultivated a community centered around authenticity and empowerment. The Aerie Influencer Program, sometimes referred to as the Aerie Real Ambassador Program, allows influencers to connect with the brand while promoting its values of inclusivity and body positivity.

Goals of the Program

*   Amplify Positive Messages: Aerie aims to spread messages of self-acceptance and body positivity through its influencers.
*   Community Building: The program fosters a sense of community among influencers who share similar values and aesthetics.
*   Product Promotion: Influencers collaborate to authentically showcase Aerie's products to their followers, thereby driving sales and brand loyalty.

How to Apply for the Aerie Influencer Program
---------------------------------------------

Getting involved in the Aerie influencer community is simple. Here's a step-by-step breakdown of the application process:

Step 1: Understand the Requirements

*   Before applying, ensure you resonate with Aerie's mission and values. Key criteria typically include:
*   A strong, engaged social media presence (preferably on Instagram, TikTok, or YouTube)
*   Authenticity in creating content that aligns with Aerie's branding
*   A passion for fashion, lifestyle, and body positivity

Step 2: Create Your Profile

*   Design a compelling profile that showcases your unique style and personality. Ensure you:
*   Use high-quality images that represent your brand well.
*   Include a comprehensive bio that communicates your values and intentions.
*   Share examples of previous collaborations or brand partnerships.

Step 3: Submit Your Application

1.   Visit the Aerie Application Page: Go to Aerie's website and navigate to their influencer program section.
2.   Fill Out the Form: Provide necessary information including social media handles, follower counts, and a brief introduction about yourself.
3.   Portfolio Submission: Include links to your previous work or campaigns relevant to Aerie’s target audience.

Step 4: Engage with the Community

Once you’ve applied, engage with Aerie’s social media accounts, utilize their hashtags, and participate in their challenges to increase visibility.
