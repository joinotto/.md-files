Why Use Google Meet Shortcuts?
------------------------------

Using keyboard shortcuts in Google Meet provides a more efficient way to manage your meetings without relying on mouse clicks. Shortcuts save time, minimize distractions, and allow you to focus more on the conversation. By familiarizing yourself with these shortcuts, you can enhance the experience for yourself and others in the meeting.

Essential Google Meet Shortcuts
-------------------------------

### General Shortcuts

*   Ctrl + Alt + C: Turn captions on/off.
*   Ctrl + D: Mute or unmute your microphone.
*   Ctrl + E: Turn video on/off.
*   Ctrl + F: Toggle fullscreen mode.

### During a Meeting

*   Ctrl + P: Open chat.
*   Ctrl + M: Toggle your microphone.
*   Ctrl + Shift + B: Raise your hand.
*   Alt + E: End the call.

### Screen Sharing Shortcuts

*   Ctrl + S: Start or stop screen sharing.
*   Ctrl + Alt + S: Share a different window/tab.

**Tip:** Practice these shortcuts a few times to memorize them for better use during meetings!

FAQs
----

### What are Google Meet shortcuts?

Google Meet shortcuts are keyboard combinations that allow users to perform different actions quickly, improving efficiency during virtual meetings.

### How do I enable captions in Google Meet?

You can enable captions by using the shortcut Ctrl + Alt + C during a meeting.

### Can I use Google Meet shortcuts on a Mac?

Yes, you can use similar shortcuts, often requiring the Command key instead of Ctrl. Check Google Meet's documentation for details.

### Are there any shortcuts for mobile users?

Google Meet also offers touch gestures for mobile devices, but they are different from keyboard shortcuts. Explore Google’s official guide for specifics.
