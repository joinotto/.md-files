### What is catch up bookkeeping?



Catch up bookkeeping is the process of reconciling and organizing financial records when you’re behind on your bookkeeping. It ensures your accounts are accurate, compliant, and ready for [tax filing](https://joinotto.com/tax-return "tax filing").

### Who is this service for?



Otto AI catch up bookkeeping services are designed for self-employed entrepreneurs and creative professionals, such as photographers, artists, podcasters, and more, who have fallen behind on their finances.

### How long does the process take?



The timeline depends on how far behind your books are. With [Otto AI automation](https://joinotto.com/automation "Otto AI Automation") and expert support, most clients see results in days, not weeks.

### How can I quickly catch up on months of bookkeeping?



Otto AI catch up bookkeeping services use AI and automation to organize backlogged data fast, turning messy records into clean, tax-ready books—so you save hours and regain control of your finances.

### What happens after my books are caught up?



After completing your catch up bookkeeping, we offer ongoing bookkeeping packages for [small businesses](https://joinotto.com/small-business-tax "Small Business") to keep your records accurate and up to date.

### Can your service handle years of unorganized receipts?



Yes, Otto AI processes years of unorganized receipts through secure uploads and AI-powered sorting. Our catch up bookkeeping services turn your financial chaos into clean, organized records—no matter how messy it starts.

### Is there human oversight alongside AI technology?



Yes. Every transaction processed by AI is double-checked by our bookkeeping experts to ensure your records are accurate and trustworthy—combining speed with precision.

### How secure is my financial data during processing?



Your data is protected with bank-level encryption and secure handling from upload to delivery. We prioritize your privacy and safeguard all sensitive information.

### Will my books be fully tax-ready after using this service?



Yes. Otto AI catch up bookkeeping services categorize transactions, verifies records with experts, and delivers reports that meet tax standards—ensuring you’re prepared when it’s time to file.

### What types of financial reports will I receive?



You'll get easy-to-understand reports covering income, expenses, and cash flow—helping you make smarter financial decisions without needing accounting experience.
