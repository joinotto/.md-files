**1. What are the eligibility requirements?**

You need a minimum of 90 followers and must reside in the United States. Engagement with your audience is also crucial.

**2. When and how will I get paid?**

Payments are processed through PayPal, and commissions are paid monthly once a minimum threshold has been reached.

**3. How long does it take for my application to be approved?**

Most applications are reviewed within 3–5 days, and you will receive a notification once your account is set up.

**4. Are there content requirements?**

Content should be honest, engaging, and related to Boots products. High-quality images and thorough descriptions enhance the likelihood of sales.

**5. What kind of support is available?**

Boots provides an affiliate dashboard for performance tracking and has a dedicated support team ready to assist with any questions or issues.
