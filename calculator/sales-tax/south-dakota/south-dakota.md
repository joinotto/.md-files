### Understanding State and Local Sales Tax in South Dakota

The base state sales tax in South Dakota is 4.2%, which is applied broadly to tangible personal property and services. However, municipalities can impose additional local sales taxes, usually adding 1% to 2%, bringing the total rate up to 6.2% or higher in some locations. This makes it essential to understand both state and local tax rates when billing customers.

*   Base state rate of 4.2%

*   Local taxes can add 1-2% more

*   Total rates up to 6.2% or higher
