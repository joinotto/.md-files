### What does the PayPal fee calculator do?



Otto AI PayPal fee calculator helps you determine exactly how much PayPal will deduct from a transaction. It shows both the amount you’ll receive after fees and the amount you need to charge to receive a specific net payment.

### How do I calculate PayPal fees manually?



Manually, you calculate fees by applying PayPal’s percentage-based fee (typically 2.9% + $0.30 for domestic U.S. transactions) to the transaction amount. Otto AI calculator automates this for faster, error-free results.

### Can I calculate reverse PayPal fees?



Yes, the calculator lets you input the exact amount you want to receive, and it tells you how much to invoice so that you receive that full amount after PayPal’s deductions.

### Does the calculator work for PayPal Goods and Services?

Absolutely. The PayPal goods and services fee calculator accounts for standard PayPal seller fees applied to transactions involving physical or digital products, services, and freelance work.
