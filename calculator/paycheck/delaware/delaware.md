### Understanding Your Delaware Paycheck

Every paycheck you receive in Delaware includes deductions that reduce your gross pay. Otto AI takes those into account to help you understand your actual earnings with precision.

*   **Federal Income Tax**

The IRS uses a progressive tax system with rates ranging from 10% to 37% depending on income and filing status. Your employer withholds this automatically from your paycheck.
*   **FICA Contributions**

These include: Social Security: 6.2% of wages (up to the annual wage base); Medicare: 1.45% of all wages; Additional Medicare Tax: 0.9% for high earners (over $200,000).
*   **Delaware State Income Tax**

Delaware imposes a graduated income tax, with rates ranging from: 2.2% to 6.6%, based on your income bracket. No local income taxes in cities like Wilmington or Dover.
*   **Pre-Tax Contributions**

You can also lower your taxable income with contributions to: 401(k) or retirement accounts; Health Savings Accounts (HSA); Flexible Spending Accounts (FSA); Insurance premiums. Otto AI Delaware tax calculator allows you to include these pre-tax deductions to get the most accurate estimate of your take-home pay.
