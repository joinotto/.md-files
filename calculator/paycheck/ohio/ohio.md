### What Goes Into Your Ohio Paycheck

Your paycheck is more than just your hourly wage or annual salary. Taxes and deductions can make a significant difference in what you take home. Otto AI simplifies the process by factoring in all the variables that affect your paycheck in Ohio.

*   **Federal Income Tax**

This is based on your income and filing status, with rates from 10% to 37%. It’s automatically withheld by your employer.
*   **FICA Taxes**

These include: Social Security: 6.2% of your wages, up to the annual wage base limit; Medicare: 1.45% of all wages; Additional Medicare Tax: 0.9% for high earners (above $200,000 for individuals).
*   **Ohio State Income Tax**

Ohio’s state income tax is graduated, with rates ranging from: 2.75% to 3.75% for most taxpayers. The state’s personal exemption and standard deduction help lower taxable income.
*   **Local Taxes in Ohio**

Many cities and school districts in Ohio also charge local income taxes: Local tax rates can range from 0.5% to 3%. These vary by municipality or school district. Otto AI Ohio tax calculator accounts for these based on your work and home address.
*   **Pre-Tax Contributions**

You can also enter pre-tax deductions like: 401(k) contributions; Health Savings Accounts (HSA); Flexible Spending Accounts (FSA); Employer-sponsored insurance. These reduce your taxable income, which Otto AI factors into your take-home pay calculation.
