### What Gets Deducted from Your New York Paycheck?

Your paycheck in New York goes through several layers of taxation before you receive your net income. Otto AI paycheck calculator for New York breaks it all down:

*   **Federal Income Tax**

This is based on your taxable income, tax bracket, and filing status, with rates ranging from 10% to 37%.
*   **FICA Taxes**

Social Security: 6.2%; Medicare: 1.45%; Additional 0.9% for higher earners (above $200,000 single / $250,000 married filing jointly)
*   **New York State Income Tax**

Ranges from 4% to 10.9%, depending on income. Multiple brackets and progressive rates.
*   **New York City and Yonkers Taxes**

NYC residents pay an additional local income tax, ranging from 3.078% to 3.876%. Yonkers residents pay a surtax of 16.75% of NY State tax owed.
*   **Pre-Tax Contributions**

You may also reduce your taxable income with contributions to: 401(k) retirement plans; Health and dental insurance premiums; Flexible Spending Accounts (FSA) or Health Savings Accounts (HSA). Otto AI NYC paycheck calculator includes all these deductions to help you determine what you actually take home.
