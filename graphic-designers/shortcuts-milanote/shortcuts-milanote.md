2. Essential Milanote Shortcuts
-------------------------------

Here’s a comprehensive list of shortcuts that every Milanote user should know, organized by their functionality to make them easy to remember.

### 2.1. Navigation Shortcuts

*   Zoom In: `Cmd + Plus` (Mac) / `Ctrl + Plus` (Windows)
*   Zoom Out: `Cmd + Minus` (Mac) / `Ctrl + Minus` (Windows)
*   Reset Zoom: `Cmd + 0` (Mac) / `Ctrl + 0` (Windows)

These shortcuts help you navigate your boards quickly without having to use the mouse, which can disrupt your flow.

### 2.2. Creating and Managing Cards

*   Add a New Card: `N`
*   Delete Selected Card: `Backspace` or `Delete`
*   Duplicate Card: `Cmd + D` (Mac) / `Ctrl + D` (Windows)

These shortcuts speed up the process of card creation and management, ensuring that you can focus on content rather than the mechanics of the platform.

### 2.3. Formatting Shortcuts

*   Bold Text: `Cmd + B` (Mac) / `Ctrl + B` (Windows)
*   Italicize Text: `Cmd + I` (Mac) / `Ctrl + I` (Windows)
*   Strikethrough Text: `Cmd + Shift + X` (Mac) / `Ctrl + Shift + X` (Windows)

Quick formatting helps maintain a professional appearance for your notes and projects without interrupting your workflow.

### 2.4. Collaboration and Share Options

*   Open Share Dialog: `Cmd + Shift + S` (Mac) / `Ctrl + Shift + S` (Windows)
*   Comment on Selected Card: `C`

Collaboration is a vital aspect of teamwork in Milanote. These shortcuts make it easier to share and get feedback from teammates.
