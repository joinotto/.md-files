Why Use Shortcuts in HitFilm?
-----------------------------

Using keyboard shortcuts in HitFilm can streamline your editing process, enabling you to:

*   Speed Up Workflow: Perform actions quickly without shifting your hands from the keyboard to the mouse.
*   Enhance Productivity: Focus on creative aspects instead of navigating through multiple menus.
*   Reduce Errors: Limit the chances of mis-clicks that can occur when using a mouse.

Essential HitFilm Shortcuts for Beginners
-----------------------------------------

### Basic Navigation Shortcuts

*   Spacebar: Play/Pause the timeline.
*   Arrow Keys: Move the playhead left or right by one frame.
*   Home/End: Jump to the start or end of the timeline.

These shortcuts help you transition between scenes effortlessly, making your initial edits smoother.

### Editing Tools Shortcuts

*   C: Cut (Split) the selected clip.
*   V: Toggle between Select and Trim tools.
*   B: Activate the Blade tool for fine cutting.

These critical shortcuts allow new users to familiarize themselves with basic editing functionality.

Advanced Shortcuts for Intermediate Users
-----------------------------------------

### Timeline Management

*   Alt + Drag: Copy a clip in the timeline.
*   Control + Z: Undo your last action, an inevitable lifesaver during any editing session.
*   Control + Shift + Z: Redo the last undone action.

This combination aids in managing your timeline more effectively, allowing for quicker and more confident editing.

### Effect Applications

*   Control + E: Opens the effect properties for the selected clip.
*   Control + A: Select all clips in the timeline for batch edits.

By leveraging these shortcuts, intermediate users can efficiently apply effects and handle multiple clips.

Expert Shortcuts for Pro Users
------------------------------

### Advanced Compositions and Keyframing

*   M: Add a marker at the playhead position.
*   Shift + T: Toggle the timeline tools menu, offering instant access to advanced editing functions.

These shortcuts are designed for users who are comfortable navigating complex projects, enabling seamless multitasking.

### Rendering and Exporting

*   Control + M: Open the Export panel.
*   Control + Alt + K: Export directly to a selected format.

Efficiently managing rendering and export processes ensures that your final product is completed and shared in a timely manner.
