Introduction
------------

The Cleartrip Affiliate Program empowers influencers to monetize their platforms by promoting travel solutions in India. With the country's vast tourism potential and growing interest in travel content, this program is strategically positioned for influencers to maximize their earnings while sharing valuable travel-related resources with their audience.

------------------

1.   **Apply**: Fill out the application form and wait for approval.
2.   **Get Trained**: Familiarize yourself with Cleartrip's offerings and audience.
3.   **Promote Effectively**: Use social media platforms to share your tailored content, ensuring alignment with current travel trends.

Strategies for Success

FAQs
----

**1. What is the eligibility criteria for the program?**

To be eligible, you must have a minimum of 40 followers on any social media platform and an active account related to travel or lifestyle.

**2. How and when will I be paid?**

Payments are made through bank transfer/UPI after reaching the minimum payout threshold, typically calculated monthly.

**3. What is the approval process like?**

Approval typically takes 3–5 days. You'll receive a confirmation email after your application is reviewed.

**4. Are there specific content requirements?**

While there are no strict content requirements, we encourage authentic and creative posts that resonate with your audience about travel-related topics.

**5. What support is available to affiliates?**

Cleartrip provides comprehensive support through a dedicated affiliate dashboard, marketing materials, and a responsive support team for any queries.
