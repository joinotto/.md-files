FAQs
----

**1. What are the eligibility requirements to join the program?**

To be eligible, you need a minimum of 250 followers and must reside in India. Content should align with Mamaearth's brand values.

**2. How are payments processed?**

Payments are processed via UPI or bank transfer, and you will receive your earnings once you reach the minimum threshold of ₹5,000.

**3. How long does the approval process take?**

The approval process typically takes 3–5 business days from the date you submit your application.

**4. What type of content is expected?**

Content should focus on promoting Mamaearth products, highlighting their benefits, and engaging your audience in an authentic manner.

**5. Is support available if I have questions?**

Yes! You will have access to a dedicated support team via email and chat for any questions or assistance you may need.
