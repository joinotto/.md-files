FAQs
----

**1. What are the eligibility requirements for the program?**

To be eligible for the Boat Brand Ambassador program, you must have a minimum follower count of 4,500. Additionally, you must be located in India with a valid bank account or UPI for payment.

**2. How do payments work in this program?**

Payments are made via bank transfer or UPI. Ensure your payment information is updated in your influencer dashboard. Payments will be processed after you reach the minimum payout threshold, which will be shared upon acceptance into the program.

**3. What is the approval process like?**

Once you apply, you can expect an approval time of 3–5 days. The program has an easy approval difficulty level, so make sure your application showcases your relevant experience and audience.

**4. Are there any specific content requirements?**

Yes, content should align with Boat's branding and aesthetic. You are encouraged to share authentic experiences and high-quality images or videos of Boat products that resonate with your audience.

**5. What support does Boat provide to influencers?**

Boat offers a dedicated support team to help you with any questions and provides marketing materials through the influencer dashboard to assist with promotions.
