FAQs
----

**Q1: What are the eligibility requirements for the Lululemon Affiliate Program?**

You need a minimum of 500 followers on any supported platform and must create content related to fitness or wellness.

**Q2: How is payment structured for affiliates?**

Affiliates earn a base commission of 0.2%, with top performers earning up to 5.2%. Payments are typically processed via PayPal.

**Q3: How long does the approval process take?**

Approval usually takes between 3 to 5 days after your application submission.

**Q4: What type of content is required?**

Content can include blog posts, social media posts, videos, and stories that feature Lululemon products and align with your personal branding.

**Q5: What support is available for affiliates?**

Affiliates have access to an affiliate dashboard, creative assets, and a dedicated support team.
