FAQs
----

**1. What are the eligibility requirements for the Indigo Affiliate Program?**

To be eligible, you must have a minimum of 80 followers and reside in the United States. Your content should resonate with Indigo's brand, and originality is key.

**2. How does the payment process work?**

Payments are processed through PayPal, typically on a monthly basis once commission thresholds are met. Ensure your PayPal account is verified to avoid delays.

**3. What is the approval process for the influencer program?**

Once you submit your application via the program page, you can expect an approval time of 3–5 days, during which the Indigo team will review your content strategy and engagement metrics.

**4. Are there specific content requirements I should follow?**

While there are no strict content guidelines, it’s important to maintain authenticity and create original, high-quality content that aligns with Indigo’s branding and products.

**5. What support is available for affiliates?**

Affiliates have access to a dedicated support team, as well as creative assets and ongoing marketing tips to help optimize performance.
