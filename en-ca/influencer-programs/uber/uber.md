**1. What are the eligibility requirements for the Uber Affiliate Program?**

To be eligible, influencers need a minimum of 20 followers on any social media platform and must comply with FTC advertising guidelines.

**2. How and when will I receive my payments?**

Payments are made via PayPal monthly, once you reach the minimum payout threshold. Ensure your PayPal account is correctly linked to receive funds.

**3. How long does the approval process take?**

The approval process takes approximately 3–5 days. Ensure your application is complete for a quicker review.

**4. Are there content requirements for affiliates?**

Yes, affiliates should create original content that adheres to FTC guidelines, promoting Uber in a positive light.

**5. What support is available to affiliates?**

Affiliates have access to a comprehensive resource hub, including promotional materials, marketing tips, and a community support network for ongoing assistance.
