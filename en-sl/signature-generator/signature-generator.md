### What is a signature generator?



A signature generator (also known as a signature maker or creator) is an online tool that allows you to draw, type, or upload your signature to create an electronic or digital signature. You can customize fonts, colors, and styles, then download it for signing Sierra Leonean business documents digitally.

### How do I use a free signature generator to create a digital signature?



With Otto AI's free signature generator, simply type or draw your name, select your preferred style and color, then download the image or embed it directly into your documents!

### Is a signature generated online legally binding?



Yes. Electronic signatures (also called e-signatures or digital signatures) created with compliant tools are legally binding in Sierra Leonean. They hold equivalent legal status to handwritten signatures under applicable Sierra Leone electronic commerce laws.

### What’s the difference between an electronic signature, e‑signature, and digital signature?



Electronic signature and e-signature are general terms for digital signing. A digital signature is a cryptographically secure type that provides tamper-proof audit trails and identity verification—ideal for important business contracts.

### Can I create a signature for free and use it for any document?



Absolutely. Free signature generators allow you to create a reusable signature (PNG, JPG) that you can apply to Word documents, PDFs, forms, contracts, and more—legally binding across numerous document types in Sierra Leonean.

### How do I create a digital signature for Word or PDF documents?



Upload your file, choose whether to type, draw, or upload your signature, position signature fields, and finalize. Most tools generate a secured signed file with a certificate or audit trail for compliance and security.

### Can I track the signing process when using an online signature generator?



Yes. Platforms like Otto AI provide real-time status updates, automated reminders, completion certificates, and comprehensive audit trails capturing signer identity, IP addresses, and timestamps—essential for Sierra Leonean business records.

### Can I use a signature creator tool on any device?



Definitely. Many online signature tools are cross-platform—web, iOS, Android, tablets, and desktops—allowing you to create, sign, and send documents from anywhere in Sierra Leonean.

### How secure are signatures created with an online signature generator?



Very secure. These platforms typically use bank-level encryption (AES 256), TLS 1.3, tamper-proof audit logs, and multi-factor authentication to ensure document integrity and identity verification.

### What are common use cases for an online signature maker or electronic signature generator?



Common use cases include signing contracts (NDAs, sales agreements), HR onboarding, vendor agreements, real estate documents, financial approvals, forms, and business workflows—particularly valuable when signers must follow a specific order or for bulk signing.
