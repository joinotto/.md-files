FAQs
----

### How can I customize a photography booking confirmation template?



You can customize a photography booking confirmation template by adding your branding, adjusting the service details, and including specific terms related to your photography packages.

### Is a photography booking confirmation legally binding?



While it serves as an agreement, a photography booking confirmation is generally not legally binding unless a formal contract is also signed. It confirms the client's booking details and mutual expectations.

### Can I use a template for different types of events?



Yes, you can adapt a photography booking confirmation template for various events like weddings, portraits, corporate events, or special occasions by adjusting the service descriptions and event specifics.

### Should I ask for a deposit in a booking confirmation?



Yes, it’s common to request a deposit within the booking confirmation to secure the booking and protect against cancellations or no-shows.

### What happens if the event details change after booking?



If event details change, the booking confirmation should be updated to reflect the new information. It’s advisable to communicate these changes clearly with the client and confirm the updated terms.
