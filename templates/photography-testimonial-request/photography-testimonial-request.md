FAQs
----

### What should I include in a testimonial request?



Be clear about why you're asking, include a simple template, and make it easy for clients to share their experience.

### When is the best time to request a testimonial?



Request a testimonial shortly after the client has received their photos, while the experience is fresh and their satisfaction is high.

### How do I phrase my testimonial request?



Use a friendly, appreciative tone. Mention specific details to guide their feedback, such as the photoshoot experience or the quality of the photos.

### What if my client doesn't respond to my testimonial request?



Follow up gently with a reminder, offering assistance if they need help writing the testimonial.

### Should I edit the client's testimonial before posting?



Avoid changing their words, but you can correct any typos or minor grammar mistakes to ensure readability.
