FAQs
----

### How do I calculate my photography quote?



Calculate your quote based on hourly rates, equipment usage, editing time, travel expenses, and licensing fees, ensuring profitability.

### Is a photography quote legally binding?



A quote itself isn’t legally binding, but once accepted and signed, it can form a contract between the photographer and client.

### How do I send a professional photography estimate?



Use an invoicing tool or a template with clear pricing, terms, and deliverables, then send it via email or PDF.

### Can I adjust my photography quote after sending it?



Yes, but communicate changes clearly with the client and get approval before finalizing the updated quote.

### What’s the difference between a quote and an invoice?



A quote is an estimate of costs before work begins, while an invoice is a request for payment after services are completed.
