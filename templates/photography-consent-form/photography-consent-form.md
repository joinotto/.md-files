FAQs
----

### Do I need a consent form for personal photoshoots?



Yes, even for personal photoshoots, it’s recommended to get consent in writing to avoid any misunderstandings about how the photos will be used.

### Can a minor sign a photography consent form?



No, a minor cannot legally sign a consent form. A parent or guardian must sign on their behalf for permission to be granted.

### What should be included in a photography consent form?



The form should include the photographer’s details, the purpose of the photoshoot, rights of the subject, and how the images will be used or distributed.

### Is a verbal consent enough for photoshoots?



No, verbal consent may not be legally binding. A written consent form provides clarity and protection for both the photographer and the subject.

### Can I revoke consent after signing the form?



Yes, you can revoke consent at any time, but it may not apply retroactively to photos already taken or used before the revocation.
