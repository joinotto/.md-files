The Shopify Affiliate Program allows influencers in Pakistan to earn commissions by promoting Shopify to their followers. With an easy application process and support from the Shopify team, influencers can monetize their content while introducing one of the leading e-commerce platforms to their audience.

Program Benefits & Perks
------------------------

*   **Generous Cookie Duration:** Enjoy a 30-day cookie life, allowing you to earn commissions on purchases made by your referrals.
*   **Diverse Payment Options:** Payments are processed via PayPal, ensuring seamless and timely transactions.
*   **Access to Resources:** Get access to promotional materials, including banners, graphics, and social media kits.

Earnings Potential
------------------

While the base commission is not explicitly defined, top performers can earn competitive rates by effectively promoting Shopify services. Earnings depend on driving sales through your unique referral link.

Tools & Support

Eligibility
-----------

To be eligible for the Shopify Affiliate Program, you must:

*   Have a minimum of 10 followers on your social media platforms.
*   Be located in Pakistan or a region where Shopify operates effectively.

Application Process
-------------------

1.   **Visit the Application Page:** Go to the [Shopify Affiliate Program page](https://joinotto.com/en-pk/influencer-programs/shopify).
2.   **Submit Your Application:** Fill out the required information about your social media presence.
3.   **Approval Time:** Expect a response within 3–5 days regarding your application status.

How to Get Started
------------------

1.   **Join the Program:** Follow the application process outlined above.
2.   **Create Quality Content:** Start promoting Shopify through engaging content on your preferred social media platforms.
3.   **Monitor Performance:** Use the tools provided to track your referral links and earnings.
