How to Get Started
------------------

1.   Submit your application through the Go City Affiliate Program link.
2.   Once approved, familiarize yourself with the provided tools and resources.
3.   Use your social media platforms to share engaging content about city experiences.

Strategies for Success
----------------------

*   **Engage with Your Audience:** Create content that resonates with your followers. Think about tips for visiting local attractions.
*   **Use Multiple Platforms:** Consider cross-promoting content on Instagram, TikTok, and your blog to maximize your reach.
*   **Stay Authentic:** Share personal experiences and honest reviews about city attractions to build trust with your audience.

FAQs
----

1.   **What are the eligibility requirements for the Go City Affiliate Program?**

You must be a US resident with a minimum of 10 followers on any social media platform. Ensure compliance with FTC guidelines regarding affiliate disclosures.
2.   **How are payments processed?**

Influencers are typically paid through PayPal once earnings reach the minimum payout threshold set by the program.
3.   **How do I know if my application was approved?**

You will receive an email within 3–5 days notifying you of your application status.
4.   **What types of content can I create for the program?**

You can create blog posts, social media stories, videos, and any engaging content that showcases city experiences and attractions.
5.   **What support is available during the program?**

Influencers will have access to a dedicated affiliate manager, a marketing toolkit, and an analytics dashboard to assist with tracking performance.
