How to Get Started
------------------

1.   Register by completing the application form.
2.   Once approved, familiarize yourself with the available resources.
3.   Start creating content featuring Uber Eats and share your unique referral link with your audience.

FAQs
----

1.   **What are the eligibility criteria for joining the program?**

To join, you need a minimum of 10 followers on any social media platform, and you must reside in the United States.
2.   **How are payments processed?**

Payments are made through PayPal, which allows for straightforward transfers once your earnings reach the minimum payout threshold.
3.   **What is the approval process like?**

The approval process is straightforward and typically takes about 3-5 days. You'll be notified via email once approved.
4.   **What kind of content is required for participation?**

Participants should create engaging content showcasing Uber Eats, including meal reviews, unboxings, or special promotions on their social platforms.
5.   **Is there support available if I have questions?**

Yes, there is a dedicated support team available to help via email, along with webinars and resources to assist you in succeeding as an influencer.
