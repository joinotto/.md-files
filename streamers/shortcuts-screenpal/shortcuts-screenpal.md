Essential ScreenPal Shortcuts
-----------------------------

### Recording Shortcuts

*   Start/Stop Recording: Press `F9` to start or stop your screen recording instantly.
*   Pause/Resume Recording: Use `F10` to pause the recording, allowing you to collect your thoughts or adjust your presentation.
*   Screenshot: Capture a quick screenshot with `Ctrl + Shift + C`.
*   Toggle Webcam: Activate or deactivate your webcam overlay by hitting `Ctrl + Shift + W`.

### Editing Shortcuts

*   Undo Action: Use `Ctrl + Z` to quickly undo mistakes during the editing phase.
*   Redo Action: Press `Ctrl + Y` to redo any action you have undone.
*   Split Video Clip: Hit `S` at the point where you want to split a clip effectively.
*   Export Video: Save your final creation with `Ctrl + E`.

### Playback Shortcuts

*   Play/Pause Video: Press the `Spacebar` to play or pause the video during playback.
*   Seek Backward: Rewind by pressing `Left Arrow`, which jumps back a few seconds.
*   Seek Forward: Fast forward by hitting `Right Arrow`.

Customizing Shortcuts
---------------------

ScreenPal allows users to customize shortcuts according to their preferences for an even more tailored experience. Follow these steps:

1.   Navigate to the settings menu in ScreenPal.
2.   Find the section labeled “Shortcuts.”
3.   Choose any shortcut you want to modify and set it to a key combination that feels intuitive to you.

This functionality enables you to create a personalized setup that can accommodate your specific workflow.
