

Invoice should include key details like an [invoice number](https://joinotto.com/invoicing/invoice-number), [date](https://joinotto.com/invoicing/invoice-date), your contact information, a brief description of the service, payment terms, [due date](https://joinotto.com/invoicing/due-date), and accepted payment methods. Using a free invoicing software helps ensure all necessary details are included.
