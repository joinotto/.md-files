

Otto AI auto categorises new bank or QuickBooks transactions in about 30 minutes, then a human bookkeeper double-checks them. Streamlined bookkeeping for small business that frees most users 5+ hours each week. ​



It blends AI automation with a creative-savvy finance team, uniting bookkeeping, invoicing, taxes, contracts and entity setup in one platform delivering end-to-end accounting for entrepreneurs with a free tier for up to $25K in annual transactions.

### Can I manage invoices and track payments easily?



Yes—deposits, milestones or recurring bills, multi currency support and smart reminders get you paid up to 30% faster, with payments syncing to your books in real time.

### How does Otto AI handle my tax preparation needs?



Otto AI tracks deductions, stores receipts, offers quarterly CPA reviews, and delivers IRS ready books while automatically generating and e-filing any required 1099s.





AI plus human reviewers tag mixed expenses so you can filter instantly clean, compliant bookkeeping for entrepreneurs and Otto AI can form an LLC or S-Corp if you still need a formal entity.

### How does a real-time dashboard help track business finances?



Within an hour of connecting accounts, live cash flow, P&L and balance sheet widgets update continuously, giving instant finance for entrepreneurs insights before month end.

### Does Otto AI offer support from real financial experts?



### What legal contract support does Otto AI provide?

You get 150+ industry specific templates, an AI assistant for drafting or summaries, built in e-signatures and direct contract to invoice linking.
